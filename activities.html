Activities to do

Water Rafting

White water rafting is an adventurous sport. For Rafting, it requires coordination and teamwork, and those who have already sat on a rubber raft with their friends and fought the primeval force of the water will never forget the experience. As a result, white water rafting vacations are becoming popular not just among freiends and family notw it is popular as a team building acitivity. Kithulgala is the ideal place for White Water Rafting in Sri Lanka.

Things included to package:

  • Changing Rooms
  • Safety lecture
  • Safety equipments
  • Boats and tools

Rates:

  • One pax - $35
  • Two pax - $30
  • Three or more pax - $20

Tuk Tuk Ride

Traveling in a Tuk Tuk increases the local vibe and helps you to fully immerse yourself in your surroundings. The Tuk Tuk safari is made even more exciting by the delicate wind blowing in from the sides, the sounds of other vehicles and the occasional wildlife, the gorgeous views, and the sensation of Sri Lankan kindness.  You'll be accompanied by a friendly Tuk Tuk driver who will explain everything you see 

Route- Through Yala East National Park, street down to Panama, Kudumgigala monastery, Okhanda Temple

  • Duration: 2h
