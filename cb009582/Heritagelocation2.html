Adam's Peak

Adam's Peak is 1.8 meters rock fromation near summit. It is regarded as a spiritual site by people of many faiths. The piligrimage season starts in Decenber and last in April. The summit is quite appealing, with densely vegetated hills all the way up, allowing for the ideal trekking experience. The mountain also serves as the headwaters for four major rivers in the country: the Kalu, Mahaweli, Kelani, and Walawe, as well as other tributaries and waterfalls. It is home to a diverse group of birds, reptiles, and mammals.

Route

There are numerous hiking trails to choose from. The longer hikes are the Kuruwita Erathna, Ihala-Maliboda, and Ratnapura routes, are more difficult and less frequently hiked, especially by tourists.
